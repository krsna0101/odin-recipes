So you'd like to make a bowl of Maggi, huh...? Just follow the steps below

Materials needed

  • A packet of Maggi
  • 1.5 cups of water
  • Salt

Now that we have the materials, let's get started

Steps

  1. Put in the 1.5 cup of water in a pan and keep the pan on high flame at a stove for the water to boil.
  2. As the water boils, add in the flavour packets and salt and stir the water well to mix up everything.
  3. Now break the pieces of uncooked Maggi into smaller chunks and put them in water and stir some more.
  4. Now put a lid over the packet and let it boil at a low flame for about 4-5 minutes.
  5. After 4-5 minutes turn the flame off and let it sit for another 5 minutes.
  6. Your dish is now ready to be served !
