So you're up for some coffee ?

Materials needed

  • Coffee packets
  • Water
  • Milk

So you're all set with the materials now, let's get started

Steps

  1. Take out milk in a pan and start heating the milk at low flame
  2. Add some water too if needed to increase the milk level
  3. Now add the coffee from the packets into the pan and keep heating and stirring till it gets a brownish color
  4. Heat at high flame and boil
  5. After some time close the flame and your coffee is just ready now.

Enjoy !
